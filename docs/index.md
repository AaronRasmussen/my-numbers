# My Numbers

My Numbers is a spreadsheet that I developed to help manage my own finances. The project also taught me how to use Apple Numbers at a high level of complexity.
The spreadsheet is available for anyone to download and use. My plan is to create a series of tutorials explaining how to use it, and a second series explaining how it was built.
My Numbers has been a valuable tool for me. I hope others will find it useful, and perhaps help it to grow and change.
